Who We Are

We're a group of young professionals living to know God and make Him known

In the summer of 2018, a few partners at Park Community Church in Chicago got together and recognized a need. While there were ministries for marriage, family, students, and every life stage and need in-between, twenty and thirty-somethings longed for a place to connect, grow in their love for God, and to encourage each other in pursuing a life rooted in Christ.

How We Do It

Worshiping & Getting to Know God

We gather to learn about and discuss topics relevant to our community and engage the issues the church is facing. We think it's also incredibly important to worship the One who has created and loved us, and pursue a saving relationship with him communally.

Connecting

It's easy to silo yourself with like-minded individuals in the industry you work in. What's amazing about God's church—let alone 3:12—is the diversity in the backgrounds and professions of its people. Paul talks about this body of believers in 1 Corinthians 12. You never know what growth in knowledge, skills, and opportunities you'll acquire with other Christians by connecting in community.

Having Fun

We love to have fun as a group! Some events we've done in the past are our Fall Festival, Friendsgiving, trivia and game nights, ski trips, and our annual Christmas Party. The sky's the limit! If you have a suggestion about what you'd like to see us do as a group, please feel free to drop us a line in the form below!

Frequently Asked Questions

Where do you get your name?

Pronounced "three-twelve," it comes from 1 Thessalonians 3:12:

May the Lord make your love increase and overflow for each other and for everyone else, just as ours does for you.

Do you have a set schedule for your events?

No. We generally have one big event per month and then any number of smaller get-togethers. These events do not have a set day as we try to accommodate different weekly schedules.

What are the themes of your events?

Our get-togethers can be spiritual, social, or both. There are no exact percentages or distribution of these kinds of events, though we try to balance them as much as possible.

How are you funded?

We fund ourselves on entrance fees and donations from our attendees. If you feel compelled to donate to our ministry, please reach out by filling out the contact form above. We thank you in advance for your kind generosity.

Isn't this a reboot of the old singles ministry?

Yes! We're re-branding it as a professionals group to be more inclusive of the young adults community at large; we encourage married couples to attend too.

Are you a dating service?

No. While we recognize that dating relationships can form out of 3:12, our sole purpose is to build community with the ultimate goal of strengthening our relationships with God.

Can I submit a suggestion for an event?

Yes, please feel free to do so with the form above. Please note that we cannot accommodate all event requests due to limited funding, available space, and volunteer staff.
